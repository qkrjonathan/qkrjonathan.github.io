I'll admit I'm having a bit of a slow start to the year. My body fat percentage has definitely gone up. I do feel like I'm getting gradually stronger, and that feels nice. But in terms of physique/aesthetics I'm not entirely where I want to be; the fact that I wasn't able to work out consistently for a few weeks before classes started up again has also been a mitigating factor.

I feel as though I'm stuck at a crossroads, between whether I should try and lean out first before bulking again, or keep gaining as much mass and physical strength while I'm on this streak. So far I'm gravitating toward the latter.
